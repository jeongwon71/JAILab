Hello, welcome to my webpage. I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Purdue University. My major is Nuclear Engineering, and my research areas cover Model validation, Sensitivity analysis, Uncertainty quantification, and Deep learning. Also, I am interested in many other things, for example, programming, reading books, watching movies, travelling around the world, etc. So, I would like to share what I know, listen to you, and develop you and myself through this website. This is my first website (maybe the last as well). Anytime you feel something to tell, please don't hesitate and feel free to contact me.

As you have seen above, I have a beautiful and even devoted wife, witty daughter and brave son. My kids are two-year and one-year old respectively, and I am really happy seeing them. I am learning parents love from them and I hope that I can give such love to them. My wife and I were married in 2013, went to Moscow in 2014, came back to Korea in 2017 with Master degree, yeah! As planned we had a baby in 2018, and came to the United States of America. In 2019, my wife delivered our son, and we completed our family. No more family member we are planning so far. But we don't know our future, right?

When I was an undergraduate, I studied Chemistry in Korean, when I was a master, Nuclear Physics in Russian, now I am studying Nuclear Engineering in English. I am not a genious, actually way far from such stuffs. I just met wonderful people, and took some lucky chances by accident. I don't know many things, but I just wish I could share some interesting things. First, I am planning to launch some projects using the image recognition technique. Also, I am planning to share my travel expreience. My wife and I travelled many countries, especially European countries.
